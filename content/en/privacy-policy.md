Bigledger Sdn Bhd ("we," "our," or "us") operates this platform. We respect your privacy rights under the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) of Malaysia. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, process, use, and share your personal data when you interact with our technology services, software applications, cloud data storage systems, or operating platforms powered by Bigledger Sdn Bhd.

## 1. Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to conduct business operations, provision enterprise software services, track cloud data utilization, and fulfill statutory legal obligations, including:

- **Identity Data**: Full name, National Registration Identity Card (NRIC) number, passport number, corporate registration numbers, or Tax Identification Number (TIN) (required for tax and LHDN compliance).
- **Contact Data**: Corporate billing address, business address, email address, and telephone numbers.
- **Transaction Data**: Details about digital payments, monthly or annual software subscriptions, dynamic cloud data scaling fees, software provisioning, applet downloads, and transaction history.
- **Technical & Usage Data**: Cloud data storage size, bandwidth consumption, IP address, access tokens, API logs, browser type, cookies, and system interaction data.

## 2. How and Why We Use Your Data

Your personal data is securely processed through the Bigledger platform for the following purposes:

- **Service Provisioning & Billing**: Activating user licenses, calculating dynamic monthly or annual cloud storage tier charges based on data size increases, configuring enterprise applets, and providing technical support.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Submitting verified billing and identity datasets to the Inland Revenue Board of Malaysia (LHDN) MyInvois portal for automated e-invoicing verification.
- **System Synchronization**: Maintaining accurate data lake integrity, multi-channel updates, security monitoring, and real-time database syncing.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosures

We do not sell your personal data. We disclose information strictly to trusted third parties required to run our technology services:

- **Infrastructure Partners**: Secure cloud hosting providers (such as Amazon Web Services) utilized to host our core data hub, store enterprise data lakes, and track data volumetric scaling.
- **Authorized Integrations**: Connected third-party applications, external APIs, or marketplaces authorized by you to interact with your Bigledger node.
- **Payment Gateways**: Licensed banking institutions, FPX providers, and B2B payment processors handling transactions.
- **Government Authorities**: The Inland Revenue Board of Malaysia (LHDN) to satisfy national e-invoicing mandates.

## 4. Data Security and Retention

Your data is protected using enterprise-grade encryption, role-based access controls, and secure cloud environments deployed by Bigledger Sdn Bhd.

To satisfy Section 82 of the Malaysian Income Tax Act 1967, transactional records, legal invoices, and corresponding tax identifiers will be securely retained for a minimum period of seven (7) years.

## 5. Your Legal Rights

Under the PDPA, you maintain the right to access your stored data, correct processing errors, or limit data processing. To exercise these rights, submit a formal request to our Data Protection Officer at [support@bigledger.com](mailto:support@bigledger.com).
