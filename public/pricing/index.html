FAQ

Common questions

Is there a free trial?
Yes. We offer a full-featured trial period so you can validate BigLedger against your real workflows before committing. Contact our team to arrange access.
Can I scale my plan up or down?
Absolutely. Plans are designed to grow with you — add modules, users, or transaction capacity as your business expands. Scaling down is equally supported.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, bank transfer, and FPX online banking. Annual contracts come with a meaningful discount over monthly billing.
Is my data secure and stored in Malaysia?
Yes. All data is stored in Malaysian data centers, encrypted at rest and in transit, with ISO 27001 certified infrastructure and daily automated backups.
What about training and onboarding?
All plans include documentation, video tutorials, and a dedicated onboarding team. Custom training sessions and data migration assistance are available based on your plan tier.
