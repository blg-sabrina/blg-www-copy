About the MDEC National E-Invoicing Initiative

MDEC — Malaysia's Peppol Authority — launched the National E-Invoicing Initiative under Pemangkin Malaysia Digital to help businesses transition smoothly to LHDN-mandated e-invoicing. The Freemium & Affordable Solutions programme partners MDEC with 16 accredited solution providers, including BigLedger, to offer Malaysian SMEs heavily discounted or free e-invoicing packages.

The initiative supports Malaysia's phased e-invoice rollout, which becomes mandatory for all businesses on 1 July 2025. BigLedger's offering is listed on the official MDEC Freemium & Affordable Solutions page.

Full Send & Receive Capability via Peppol

BigLedger is a 4-corner Peppol Access Point — both outbound (sending) and inbound (receiving) document flows are fully supported, in line with MDEC and OpenPeppol specifications.

📤

Sending (Outbound)

  • Issue compliant e-invoices in UBL 2.1 / PINT MY format from BigLedger's web app, mobile app, or API.
  • LHDN MyInvois submission via certified API integration with automated TIN validation, signing, and QR-code embedding.
  • Peppol BIS Billing 3.0 / MY PINT dispatch to any Peppol-registered counterparty domestically and across borders.
  • Document types: Invoice, Credit Note, Debit Note, Self-billed Invoice, Refund Note.
  • Real-time delivery status with automated retry, MLR (Message Level Response) handling, and exception alerts.
📥

Receiving (Inbound)

  • Receive Peppol documents from any 4-corner Access Point worldwide, routed to your BigLedger inbox.
  • Automatic validation against PINT MY / Peppol BIS schematron rules with structured error reporting back to the sender.
  • Inbound document types: Invoice, Credit Note, Debit Note, Order, Order Response, Despatch Advice.
  • Auto-matching with purchase orders and goods receipts, with one-click posting into Accounts Payable.
  • Peppol Directory registration (SMP) handled by BigLedger so trading partners can discover and route to your Peppol ID.

BigLedger Peppol ID format: 0230:<your-LHDN-TIN> (Malaysia scheme). Both production and pre-production (test) environments are operated and monitored 24/7.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this promotion really free? Any hidden charges?

Yes, genuinely free for 3 years on up to 200 invoice transactions per month. No setup fees, no credit card required. This is part of BigLedger's commitment under the MDEC National E-Invoicing Initiative to help Malaysian SMEs adopt e-invoicing.

What counts as a "transaction"?

One transaction = one invoice, credit note, or debit note successfully submitted to LHDN's MyInvois portal. Drafts, validations, and re-submissions of the same document do not count.

What happens after 3 years, or if I exceed 200/month?

You can seamlessly upgrade to a paid BigLedger plan at standard pricing. Your data, settings, and history carry over — no migration required.

Does this cover PEPPOL cross-border invoicing?

Yes. BigLedger is an MDEC-accredited Peppol Access Point with full 4-corner send and receive capability, so you can issue and receive invoices across the international Peppol network within your 200/month allowance.

Can BigLedger both send and receive invoices via Peppol?

Yes — BigLedger operates a full 4-corner Peppol Access Point. Sending: issue invoices, credit notes, debit notes, self-billed invoices and refund notes in UBL 2.1 / PINT MY format to LHDN MyInvois and to any Peppol counterparty worldwide. Receiving: accept inbound invoices, credit notes, debit notes, orders, order responses and despatch advices from any Peppol Access Point, with automatic validation, MLR feedback, and AP auto-matching.

What support do I get during and after onboarding?

The promotion includes the full BigLedger support package at no extra cost: a dedicated onboarding specialist (typical go-live in 3 business days), technical assistance from our Malaysia-based engineering team, live and on-demand training in English / Bahasa Malaysia / Mandarin, multi-channel helpdesk (email, in-app, Telegram, WhatsApp) with 4-hour business-hour response and 24/7 critical escalation, plus automatic compliance updates whenever LHDN or MDEC publishes new rules.

Can I integrate my existing accounting system?

Absolutely. BigLedger provides REST APIs, SFTP batch imports, and CSV/Excel upload options. Popular integrations are available out of the box.

Is my data stored in Malaysia?

Yes. BigLedger hosts data in Malaysian data centres, compliant with PDPA and LHDN's 7-year retention rules.
