Applets & Workflows

BigLedger’s modular architecture is built on applets — reusable components that provide specific business functionality. Each applet can be used by multiple modules, ensuring consistency across your entire system.

Reusable

One applet serves multiple modules.

Configurable

Customize to your business needs.

Integrated

Applets work together seamlessly.

Independent

Updated without affecting others.

Many-to-many by design: applets are shared across modules. For example, the Tax Configuration Applet is used by the Financial Accounting, Sales, Purchasing, and E-Commerce modules — one applet, one source of truth, consistent behavior everywhere.
